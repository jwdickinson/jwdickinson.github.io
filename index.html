About Me

I am a Multilingual Data Scientist from the Greater Chicago Area, IL with dual master’s degrees in Computational and Hispanic Linguistics (4.0 GPA). Specializing in NLP, LLMs, and MLOps, I bring deep expertise in transforming unstructured text into actionable business insights. Throughout my career, I’ve built end-to-end machine learning pipelines and delivered AI-driven solutions for product development, internal enablement, and C-suite decision-making.

My journey from linguistics academia to industry taught me how to tackle the unknown. I push through uncertainty by embracing constant learning and iteration with a positive mindset. Whether it’s building RAG-based AI tools to improve engagement or fine-tuning LLMs for an enterprise product, my focus is on driving real-world impact. I love collaborating with cross- functional teams, communicating complex concepts to non-technical audiences, and bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and practical solutions.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring the intersection of linguistics and AI, doing CrossFit, learning new languages, and creating approachable learning materials to data science over on Substack.
